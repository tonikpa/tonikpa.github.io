OIKOS
⬇

Oikos is a work in progress game/experience. Heavily inspired by Mark Z Danielewski’s The House of Leaves and Jorge Luis Borges’ The House of Asterion, the game traps the player in an infinite maze of rooms, and the objective is finding a way out.

The game relies on procedural generation to evoke feelings of endlessness and imprisonment, creating architecturally impossible maze. If walking in the wrong direction, the player will find themselves encountering an infinite set of rooms and doors. It uses its minimalist art style, paired with custom fadable shaders, to create a meditative experience and contrast with the maximalism of an infinite house.
