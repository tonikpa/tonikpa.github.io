THIS JOURNEY

This Journey is a game made in a weekend. With it's art style that constrasts simple flat pastel characters and flowers with the oppressive industrial mega structures in the background, This Journey is a playable poem about losing and finding love through life.

In this project I did most parts of the development, expect I borrowed industrial photographs for the backgrounds and Godspeed You! Black Emperor's Storm for the soundtrack.
