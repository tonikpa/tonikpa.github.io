ODE TO AMERICAN FOOTBALL

Ode to American Football is a game made in two weeks. It is a silly satirical game about the last 100 years of American national and international politics. A game about the USA made by a Brazilian. About excess, history, cultural imperialism, and patriotism.

In this project I did most parts of the development, except I borrowed a bunch of music, sounds and textures.
