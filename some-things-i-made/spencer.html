SPENCER (FROM THE HIT 2007 NICKELODEON TEEN SITCOM ICARLY)'S ULTIMATE RAT RACE!

Spencer (from the hit 2007 Nickelodeon teen sitcom iCarly)'s Ultimate Rat Race! is a game made in two weeks. It is an comedic offbeat game about collecting rats. It plays with the eccentric nature of early 2000s television and videogame scene. It's an exploration on comedy writing and creating simultaneously eerie and funny characters and spaces.

In this project I did most parts of the development, expect I borrowed textures, sfx and models from old games.
