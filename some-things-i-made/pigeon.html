PIGEON LADY

Pigeon Lady is a game made in two weeks. It is a chaotic collection of simultaneous minigames depicting the ever-reapeating routine of the Pigeon Lady. This game is an exploration of the sisyphean aspects of routine, but also of its therapeutic properties. This game also allowed me to explore a new paper-cut-out-esque and different ways to tell a narrative.

In this project I did all parts of the development.
