THE KING OF WANDS

The King of Wands is a game made in a weekend. It's a game about a boy that finds some cool sticks and tries to show them around. It allowed me to explore an art style of a heavy contrast between simple characters and decaying ruinous spaces. It is an exploration on being a kid learning how to communicate with people. It's about being known, about showing your toy, about being aggressive because you don't have a lot of verbs yet.

In this project I did all parts of the development.
